I am currently a fourth year PhD Candidate in Computer Science at University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), advised by Professor Omid Abari.

My current research interest is in AI for Wireless systems, Internet of Things and Integrated Sensing and Communication.

Before joining UCLA, I received a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering with highest honor from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), and a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical Engineering and Automation from Zhejiang University. It’s a dual-degree undergrad program by ZJU-UIUC Institute.
